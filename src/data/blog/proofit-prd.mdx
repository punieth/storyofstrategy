  <section class="proof-section" id="product">
    <div class="proof-section-heading">
      <p class="proof-label">04 / Product Flow</p>
      <h2>A credibility link for the exact WhatsApp moment.</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="proof-loop">
      <article>
        <span>1</span>
        <h3>Referral &amp; Details Ask</h3>
        <p>A customer discovers a vendor via word-of-mouth and starts a WhatsApp chat, asking: "Can you send your details?"</p>
      </article>
      <article>
        <span>2</span>
        <h3>Vendor Shares Profile</h3>
        <p>The vendor replies with a single ProofIt link that packages their registration, portfolio, pricing, and client feedback.</p>
      </article>
      <article>
        <span>3</span>
        <h3>Review &amp; Quote Request</h3>
        <p>The customer reviews recent projects and reviews, selects services, and requests a custom quote through the profile.</p>
      </article>
      <article>
        <span>4</span>
        <h3>WhatsApp Follow-Up</h3>
        <p>The quote request automatically routes back to the vendor's WhatsApp, keeping the conversational momentum alive to close the sale.</p>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="proof-not-box">
      <h3>What ProofIt is not</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Not a marketplace. We don't match buyers and vendors; we help close deals after discovery.</li>
        <li>Not another directory. No listings, paid ads, or search rankings. We are a dedicated proof page.</li>
        <li>Not a website builder. No complex layouts or design headaches; just a clean, professional profile.</li>
        <li>Not a CRM. We don't manage customer relationships; we just bridge the post-discovery credibility gap.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="proof-section" id="design-system">
    <div class="proof-section-heading">
      <p class="proof-label">07 / Design Decisions</p>
      <h2>Product-first design built for real-world B2B workflows</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="proof-vouch-grid">
      <article class="proof-card">
        <p class="proof-card-label">Product Design</p>
        <h3>Mobile-first over WhatsApp</h3>
        <p>Since profiles are shared inside WhatsApp chats, the interface must load instantly on mobile, prioritizing quick readability over desktop density.</p>
      </article>
      <article class="proof-card proof-card--dark">
        <p class="proof-card-label">Product Design</p>
        <h3>Visual proof first</h3>
        <p>Buyers evaluate work quality and verified client reviews before discussing pricing. The hierarchy places recent project photos front and center.</p>
      </article>
      <article class="proof-card">
        <p class="proof-card-label">Technology</p>
        <h3>Lightweight stack</h3>
        <p>Built with React (Vite) and Tailwind CSS to guarantee sub-second page loads on slow mobile connections, preventing drop-offs during live chat.</p>
      </article>
    </div>
  </section>
