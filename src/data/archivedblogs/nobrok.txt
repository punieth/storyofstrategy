6. Pain Point 1 ‚Äî "The Silence Window (D0‚ÄìD3)"

Real User Quote (Trustpilot): "I listed my property, refreshed ten times, and saw nothing. No views, no calls, nothing. How do I know if my listing is even visible?"

13. RISKS, EDGE CASES & MITIGATIONS

Risk 1: Low-Traffic Localities (Discouraging Zeros)
Scenario: Owner in Tier-3 city sees "0 views" for 5 days ‚Üí increased anxiety
Mitigation:
‚Ä¢ City-level benchmarking: "Typical for [city] properties to get 3-5 views/week"
‚Ä¢ Honest copy: "Your area has lower demand‚Äîconsider adjusting rent by 10% or expanding search radius"
‚Ä¢ Proactive RM outreach: Call after 7 days of 0 activity (genuine help, not spam)
‚Ä¢ Success metric: <5% increase in support tickets from low-traffic areas

Risk 2: Owners Obsess Over Metrics (Anxiety Amplification)
Scenario: Visibility Tracker backfires‚Äîowners check 10√ó per day, become more anxious
Mitigation:
‚Ä¢ Throttle notifications: Max 1 push notification per day (not real-time alerts)
‚Ä¢ Encouraging language: "On track," "warming up," "typical for your area"
‚Ä¢ Avoid gamification: No "boost your listing" upsells in Visibility Tracker
‚Ä¢ Monitor: If dashboard visits >10√ó/day, add friction ("Check back tomorrow for updates")

Risk 3: Recommendations Are Wrong (Trust Erosion)
Scenario: "Add balcony photo" recommendation, but owner has no balcony ‚Üí frustration
Mitigation:
‚Ä¢ Conservative recommendations: Only suggest actions with >80% confidence
‚Ä¢ Generic advice: "Listings with 8+ photos perform better" (not prescriptive)
‚Ä¢ Feedback loop: "Was this helpful?" thumbs up/down on each recommendation
‚Ä¢ Iterate: Use feedback to improve recommendation engine over time

Risk 4: Real-Time Infrastructure Costs Spike
Scenario: 200K owners checking dashboard 6√ó/day = 1.2M requests/day ‚Üí 50% cost increase
Mitigation:
‚Ä¢ Start with 1-hour batch updates (acceptable for MVP)
‚Ä¢ Optimize later: Redis cache, CDN for static benchmarking data
‚Ä¢ Monitor: Track server costs weekly‚Äîif >20% increase, throttle polling frequency
‚Ä¢ Fallback: Degrade to daily updates if infrastructure can't scale

Edge Case Handling:

---------|-----------------|------------------
